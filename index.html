How it works

AI Clipper runs entirely on your own computer. You stay in control of every account and every clip.

1

Connect your accounts. Sign in with TikTok (Login Kit) or YouTube (Google OAuth) so AI Clipper knows which account to publish to. You authorize each account yourself and can revoke access at any time.

2

Import a source video. Load a long-form video from your device into the app.

3

Let AI Clipper find the best clips. The app analyzes the video locally and generates short, edited clips with subtitles and on-screen text.

4

Review and publish. Preview each clip, then publish it directly to your connected TikTok or YouTube account through the official API.

Your data & permissions

AI Clipper is a local tool. We do not run servers and we do not collect your data.

What we access on TikTok

  • user.info.basic — your display name, avatar and open ID, to show which account is connected.
  • video.publish — to post a clip directly to your own profile, only when you ask.
  • video.upload — to send a clip to your account as a draft you can finish in TikTok.

Where your data lives

Access tokens and account info are stored only on your device, in the app's local data folder. Videos are processed on your machine and uploaded directly to TikTok — never through a server of ours.

You stay in control

You connect and disconnect accounts yourself. You can revoke AI Clipper's access anytime in TikTok Settings → Security → Manage app permissions.

No third-party sharing

We never sell or share your data. The only service your content is sent to is TikTok, as a direct result of an action you take in the app.

Frequently asked questions

Is AI Clipper a website or an app?

AI Clipper is a desktop application that runs on your own computer. This site is its official information page, and hosts the OAuth redirect used when you connect a TikTok account.

Does my video get uploaded to your servers?

No. Videos are processed locally and uploaded directly from your device to TikTok through the official Content Posting API. We operate no servers.

How do I disconnect my TikTok account?

Inside the app, remove the account, or revoke access from TikTok Settings → Security → Manage app permissions. The stored token becomes invalid immediately.
