AI Clipper

An AI-powered desktop tool that turns your long-form videos into short clips and publishes them directly to your own TikTok and Youtube account.

AI clip selection Auto subtitles Direct TikTok publishing
✦

AI clip selection

Automatically detects the most engaging moments in a long video and turns them into ready-to-post short clips.

✦

Automatic subtitles

Generates transcription and styled subtitles for every clip, no manual editing required.

✦

Template-based editing

Uses customizable templates and FFmpeg to render clips in the format and style creators expect on TikTok and Youtube.

✦

Direct TikTok and Youtube publishing

Connect your accounts and publish edited clips straight from the app, on your own schedule.

How it works

1

Connect your TikTok account. Sign in with TikTok (Login Kit) so AI Clipper knows which account to publish to.

2

Import a source video. Load a long-form video from your device into the app.

3

Let AI Clipper find the best clips. The app analyzes the video and generates short, edited clips with subtitles.

4

Review and publish. Preview each clip in the app, then publish it directly to your connected Youtube and TikTok profile.
