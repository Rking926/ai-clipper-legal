Privacy Policy

Application: AI Clipper  |  Last updated: June 2026

Short version: AI Clipper is a local desktop application. Your data never leaves your own computer except for the direct API calls you initiate to TikTok's servers. We do not operate servers, collect analytics, or share any data with third parties.

1. Who We Are

AI Clipper is a desktop application developed and operated by Xdark (independent digital consultant), France.
Contact: contact@xdark.fr  

2. What AI Clipper Does

AI Clipper is an automated video production and social media publishing tool. It:

  • Processes video files locally on your device using FFmpeg.
  • Generates transcriptions and AI-based editing suggestions locally (via Whisper and Ollama).
  • Connects to TikTok's API on your behalf to publish videos to accounts you have authorized.
  • Retrieves performance metrics from TikTok for videos you have published through the app.
  • Stores your TikTok OAuth access tokens locally on your device.

3. Data We Collect and Process

3.1 TikTok Account Data

When you connect a TikTok account through the OAuth 2.0 authorization flow, we store locally:

  • Your TikTok access token and refresh token (encrypted on disk).
  • Your TikTok display name, username, and profile picture URL (for display in the app interface).
  • Open ID assigned by TikTok to identify your account.

This data is stored only on your local device in the application's data folder (backend/data/). It is never transmitted to our servers because we do not operate any.

3.2 Video Content

Video files you add to AI Clipper are processed entirely on your local machine. The resulting edited clips are uploaded directly from your device to TikTok's servers via TikTok's Content Posting API. We do not transmit your video content to any intermediate server operated by us.

3.3 Performance Metrics

After publishing, AI Clipper requests performance data (views, likes, comments, shares) from TikTok's API for videos you have published through the app. This data is stored locally to help you track results. It is not shared with any third party.

3.4 Local Application Logs

The application maintains local log files (backend.log, frontend.log) to help diagnose errors. These logs remain on your device. They may contain API response codes and timestamps but do not contain the content of your videos or access tokens.

4. How We Use Your Data

All data processed by AI Clipper is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok on your behalf to publish content you have selected.
  • Display your account information within the local application interface.
  • Show you performance analytics for your published videos.
  • Enable scheduling and automation of your TikTok publishing workflow.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond operating the application as described above.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party.

The only external service your data is sent to is TikTok, as a direct result of your intentional actions within the app (connecting an account, publishing a video, requesting metrics). TikTok's own Privacy Policy governs how TikTok handles that data on their end.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All application data is stored in the backend/data/ folder on your local device.
  • Access tokens are stored in local JSON files. You are responsible for the physical security of your device.
  • No cloud backup, no remote database, no third-party storage service is used.

7. Your Rights and Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Revoke TikTok access: Go to TikTok Settings → Security → Manage App Permissions to revoke AI Clipper's access at any time. The stored token will become invalid.
  • Delete local data: Delete the backend/data/ folder to remove all locally stored account information.
  • Request information: Contact us at contact@xdark.fr for any questions about your data.

8. Children's Privacy

AI Clipper is a professional tool intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has used the application, please contact us immediately.
