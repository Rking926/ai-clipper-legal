Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2025

AI Clipper ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect social media accounts (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram) to AI Clipper, we collect:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens required to publish content on your behalf
  • Basic profile information (username, display name, profile picture) to identify accounts
  • Video performance metrics (views, likes, comments) from your published posts

We do not collect passwords. Authentication is handled entirely through each platform's official OAuth 2.0 flow.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To publish videos to your connected social media accounts at scheduled times
  • To retrieve performance statistics on your published content
  • To improve clip selection and caption generation based on your results

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. TikTok Data

AI Clipper uses the TikTok Content Posting API solely to publish short-form video content on behalf of users who have explicitly authorized the application. We access only the scopes required for posting and reading basic creator information. We do not access your TikTok messages, contacts, or private data.

TikTok data obtained through the API is used exclusively to operate the publishing feature and is not shared with any third party. Data is stored locally on the user's own machine and is not uploaded to any external server operated by AI Clipper.

4. Data Storage

All data (tokens, video metadata, metrics) is stored locally on your own machine in the application's data directory. We do not operate external servers that store your data.

5. Data Retention

Tokens and account data are retained until you explicitly disconnect an account within the application. You may delete all stored data at any time by removing the application's data directory.

6. Third-Party Services

AI Clipper integrates with the following platforms through their official APIs. Your use of these platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • TikTok — tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • YouTube / Google — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Instagram / Meta — privacycenter.instagram.com/policy

7. Children's Privacy

AI Clipper is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
