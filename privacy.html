Privacy Policy

Application: AI Clipper  |  Last updated: July 2026

Short version: AI Clipper is a local desktop application. Your data never leaves your own computer except for the direct API calls you initiate to TikTok's and YouTube's servers. We do not operate servers, collect analytics, or share any data with third parties.

1. Who We Are

AI Clipper is a desktop application developed and operated by an independent developer based in France.
Contact: raphaelmoquet1882@gmail.com

2. What AI Clipper Does

AI Clipper is an automated video production and social media publishing tool. It:

  • Processes video files locally on your device using FFmpeg.
  • Generates transcriptions and AI-based editing locally (via Whisper and Ollama).
  • Connects to TikTok's and YouTube's APIs on your behalf to publish videos to accounts you have authorized.
  • Stores your OAuth access tokens locally on your device.

3. Data We Collect and Process

3.1 TikTok Account Data

When you connect a TikTok account through the OAuth 2.0 authorization flow, we store locally:

  • Your TikTok access token and refresh token.
  • Your TikTok display name, username, and profile picture URL (for display in the app interface).
  • The Open ID assigned by TikTok to identify your account.

This data is stored only on your local device in the application's data folder. It is never transmitted to our servers because we do not operate any.

3.2 Video Content

Video files you add to AI Clipper are processed entirely on your local machine. The resulting edited clips are uploaded directly from your device to TikTok's servers via TikTok's Content Posting API (using the video.publish and video.upload scopes). We do not transmit your video content to any intermediate server operated by us.

3.3 Local Application Logs

The application maintains local log files to help diagnose errors. These logs remain on your device. They may contain API response codes and timestamps but do not contain the content of your videos or your access tokens.

4. How We Use Your Data

All data processed by AI Clipper is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok on your behalf to publish content you have selected (video.publish, video.upload).
  • Display your account information within the local application interface (user.info.basic).
  • Enable scheduling and automation of your publishing workflow.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond operating the application as described above.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party.

The only external services your data is sent to are TikTok and YouTube, as a direct result of your intentional actions within the app (connecting an account, publishing a video). TikTok's own Privacy Policy governs how TikTok handles that data on their end.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • All application data is stored in the app's local data folder on your device.
  • Access tokens are stored in local files. You are responsible for the physical security of your device.
  • No cloud backup, no remote database, no third-party storage service is used.

7. Your Rights and Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Revoke TikTok access: TikTok Settings → Security → Manage app permissions, to revoke AI Clipper's access at any time. The stored token then becomes invalid.
  • Delete local data: delete the application's data folder to remove all locally stored account information.
  • Request information: contact us at raphaelmoquet1882@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

AI Clipper is a professional tool intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
